Hello! I'm an electrical engineering graduate from SUST with interests in Computer Vision, Robotics and Wearables.

I enjoy tackling complex open ended problems by combining algorithms with practical hardware solutions, from 3D perception and point cloud processing to wearable assistive technologies.

I also love fine arts, instrumental music (singing occasionally) and old hardware. Do check out the rest of my pages in the portfolio!
